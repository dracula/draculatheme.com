Ulysses

Ulysses Style Exchange

You can get Dracula from the Ulysses Style Exchange.

Install manually

Clone or download the repo, then open the Dracula.ultheme file.

Activating theme

  1. Open Ulysses' preferences (Ulysses > Preferences...)
  2. Click Markup
  3. Choose Dracula in the list of themes at the bottom.
