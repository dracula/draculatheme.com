A couple of days ago, I was configuring AWS and noticed they’re using Dracula. Even Amazon is using Dracula.

![Dracula Theme in AWS](/images/blog/aws-new-wallpapers-and-five-new-themes-a.webp)

For us on the Dracula team, wallpapers are something meticulously chosen. After all, they keep the vampiric aesthetic and add personality to our machines.

With that in mind, we updated the wallpaper pack with a [new collection](https://github.com/dracula/wallpaper/tree/master/second-collection), including this one:

![A Dracula Theme Wallpaper from the new collection](/images/blog/aws-new-wallpapers-and-five-new-themes-b.webp)

## New on Dracula

Meet the five new themes that have joined Dracula recently:

- [New Tabs](/new-tabs): **Make your new tab yours.** An extension to customize your new tab page in Chrome and Firefox.
- [Infinity for Reddit](/infinity-for-reddit): **A smoother Reddit scroll.** A more beautiful, feature-rich Reddit experience.
- [Fedilab](/fedilab): **Your fediverse client.** An app for exploring the distributed fediverse.
- [Harpy for Twitter](/harpy-for-twitter): **A chronological timeline.** An app to browse Twitter in order, with a cleaner experience.
- [Nitter](/nitter): **Lightweight and open source.** A free, open-source alternative front end for Twitter.

Special thanks to [@vkeerthivikram](https://github.com/vkeerthivikram), who contributed 3 of these themes! 💘

[![Dracula theme for Harpy for Twitter](/images/blog/aws-new-wallpapers-and-five-new-themes-c.webp)](/harpy-for-twitter)
