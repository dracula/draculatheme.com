## Highlights

Many people asked if the Dracula Pro package included the color codes for them to use in other apps.

Because of that, we decided to not only describe all colors in _HEX_, _RGB_, and _HSL_, but also add some design files for:

- Adobe Photoshop & Illustrator
- Figma
- Sketch
