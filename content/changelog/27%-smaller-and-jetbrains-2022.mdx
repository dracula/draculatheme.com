### Highlights

This update removes videos from the Dracula Pro package, reducing the download size by 27% and making updates faster for users.

![Dracula Pro’s on JetBrains IDE’s](/images/logs/27%-smaller-and-jetbrains-2022-a.png)

### JetBrains update

JetBrains released version **2022.1**. If you use IntelliJ, PyCharm, CLion, DataGrip, WebStorm, or RubyMine, update your Dracula Pro plugin to the latest version.
