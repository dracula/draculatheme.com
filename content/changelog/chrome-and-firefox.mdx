## Highlights

This update introduces Dracula Pro themes for the world’s most popular browsers, bringing consistent theming to your entire browsing experience.

## Chrome

All six Dracula Pro variants are now available for Google Chrome, the world’s most used browser.

## Firefox

Mozilla Firefox now supports all Dracula Pro variants with accent colors on active tabs and buttons for a polished, cohesive look.
