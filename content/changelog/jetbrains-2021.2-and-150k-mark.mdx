### Highlights

This update adds support for JetBrains **2021.2**. If you use IntelliJ, PyCharm, WebStorm, CLion, DataGrip, or RubyMine, update your Dracula Pro plugin.

We’ve reached $151,328.16 in sales. We’ve shared lessons learned in a [Twitter thread](https://twitter.com/zenorocha/status/1420744224417353729) and updated the [Dracula Pro Journey](/pro/journey) page with insights from this milestone.
