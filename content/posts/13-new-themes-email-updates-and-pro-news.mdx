Today I'm writing from San Francisco, where my car just got stolen.

It's been a while since I don't send these Dracula updates, right? The main reason is that I've been building a new email platform - [Resend](https://resend.com).

So far, Resend has only been used to send transactional emails, but we're now adding the ability to send broadcast emails.

This is still under a feature flag, but I'm happy to say that all Dracula emails are no longer being sent via Mailchimp and are now fully migrated to Resend.

## ‚ú® New on Dracula

Today is not Friday the 13th, but 13 new themes have been added! Meet them:

- [**Audacity**](https://draculatheme.com/audacity): an **easy-to-use, multi-track audio editor** and recorder;
- [**Blockbench**](https://draculatheme.com/blockbench): a **low-poly 3D model editor**;
- [**exa**](https://draculatheme.com/exa): a more **user-friendly** version of ls;
- [**ForkLift**](https://draculatheme.com/forklift): a dual pane **file manager** and **file transfer** client;
- [**iSH**](https://draculatheme.com/ish): a **Linux shell** for iOS;
- [**Jupyter Notebook**](https://draculatheme.com/jupyter-notebook): a **web-based interactive computing** platform;
- [**Kurozora**](https://draculatheme.com/kurozora): an app to **keep track** of your ***Anime* and games** played;
- [**lsd**](https://draculatheme.com/lsd): a **rewrite of GNU ls**, with new features;
- [**man-pages**](https://draculatheme.com/man-pages): an interface to the Linux system reference manuals;
- [**QTerminal**](https://draculatheme.com/qterminal): a lightweight **Qt-based terminal** emulator;
- [**SpeedCrunch**](https://draculatheme.com/speedcrunch): a **high-precision scientific** calculator;
- [**tig**](https://draculatheme.com/tig): a **text-mode interface** for Git;
- [**vis**](https://draculatheme.com/vis): a **vi-like editor based** on Plan 9's **regular expressions**.
